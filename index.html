FAQs

Common questions about our services.

How quickly can you deliver?

Most urgent deliveries are scheduled within 24–48 hours. Routine material supply runs are coordinated on a weekly or monthly schedule based on your project needs.

What equipment do you hire?

We offer excavators, loaders, compactors, concrete mixers, and site-support machinery. All equipment is maintained to industry standards and available short or long term.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We work with project managers on flexible invoicing, deposit schedules, and payment terms suited to your project phase and cash flow.

Can you support a full house build?

Absolutely. We coordinate logistics, supply materials, hire equipment, and manage labour coordination from foundation to final handover.

What areas do you serve?

We operate in urban, suburban, and rural areas. Call or email to confirm your location and we will confirm availability and lead times.

How do I get a detailed quote?

Use the enquiry form or contact us directly by phone or WhatsApp. Share your project scope and we will send a customised proposal within one working day.
